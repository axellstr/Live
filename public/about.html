A passionate car detailing expert turning vehicles into immaculate works of art.

Hello! I'm Stefanos, the founder and owner of McQueen's Detailing. With over a decade of experience in the automotive care industry, I've dedicated my career to perfecting the art of car detailing. What started as a passion project in my garage has grown into a renowned detailing service trusted by car enthusiasts across the region.

At McQueen's Detailing, we believe that every vehicle deserves meticulous care and attention. Our team of skilled professionals uses cutting-edge techniques and premium products to restore and enhance your car's appearance, inside and out. From paint correction and ceramic coatings to interior deep cleaning and leather restoration, we offer a comprehensive range of services tailored to meet the unique needs of each vehicle and owner.

McQueen's Detailing Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a premier car detailing service, McQueen's Detailing has consistently delivered excellence in automotive care.

2018 - Present
Expansion and Innovation
@ McQueen's Detailing

We've expanded our services to include ceramic coatings and paint protection films. Our team has grown to 10 expert detailers, and we've opened a second location to meet growing demand. McQueen's has become the go-to detailer for luxury and exotic car owners in the region.

2015 - 2018
Growth and Recognition
@ McQueen's Detailing

Our reputation for quality work led to rapid growth. We moved to a larger facility, invested in state-of-the-art equipment, and expanded our service offerings. McQueen's won local business awards and gained recognition in auto enthusiast circles.

2012 - 2015
Founding Years
@ McQueen's Detailing

Stefanos founded McQueen's Detailing in his home garage, offering basic detailing services. With a focus on quality and customer satisfaction, the business quickly gained a loyal following. By 2015, we had our first dedicated detailing shop and two full-time employees.

About McQueen's Detailing
